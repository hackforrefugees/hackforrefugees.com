Hello, good to see you here!

Currently there are several millions of people that have been forced to flee from their home countries and become refugees. Sweden is one of the countries that take in most refugees per capita, this year 140-150 thousand refugees are expected to arrive in Sweden. This is a huge increase from previous years, causing major challenges for the organisations trying to help the refugees. Several of these challenges can be solved with IT.

Proposed Ideas

We have a list of proposed ideas that organisations and individuals have handed to us, see some examples below or check them all out here.

  • Reunite

    A site dedicated to refugees who accidentally got away from friends or family during their escape, providing them help to find their loved ones again.

  • Refugee Event

    A web application where people who arrange activities for refugees can add their events, as well as a place where refugees can get information about upcoming events.

  • Blending Matching

    A platform providing possibilities to match families (refugee families with swedish families) that wants to do fun stuff together.

  • Refugee Phones Support System

    A web application to support Refugee Phones in their awesome work of delivering phones to the ones who needs it the most. The application could for example support logging of the in- and outflow of phones, donations from companies or individuals, as well as functionality for ordering phones for refugees.

  • 400contacts Instant Messaging

    A platform for connecting refugees with local professionals and let mentors and and adepts communicate easily though instant messaging. This as a way to help refugees with engineering background get their first skilled job in Sweden.

  • Scheduling Volunteers

    An app for scheduling volunteers at refugee homes and a place where volunteers can register that they’re willing to help. Security is a recurring problem at refugee homes, so this registration needs to have some kind of strong authentication, preferable bank-id or something similar.

The hack

On the 5-6 of December we are organizing Hack for Refugees, where we will gather a large group of programmers, designers and other tech people. Together we will try to build as many tools as possible that could be helpful for both the refugees and the volunteers.

Our goal is to increase the awareness of the problems the refugees face and find new ways for people to help.

Frequently asked Questions

When can we start hacking?

Whenever you want! If you want to get going right away, just send us a message and tell us what you want to work on so we can make sure no teams build the same solution. But the actual hack starts at Burt on Saturday the 5th of December at 09:00. You can keep hacking there until 19:00 on Sunday the 6th, when you’ll get to show off what you’ve done!

Will there be food?

Hell’s yeah. Breakfast, lunch and dinner both days!

I don’t know what to hack on!

Don’t worry! Just bring a towel and have a look at the cases section.

We want to hack as a team, is that possible?

It is! Either have the teammates enter the same team name when registering, or just show up and sit next to each other on hack day :)

Will there be prizes?

Karma. Loads. And possible something more tangible as well.
